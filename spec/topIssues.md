The concept site has a clean, simple structure and a consistent visual theme, but it currently feels unfinished, generic, and lacking in credibility signals compared to real footwear brands like Clarks, Schuh, Nike, Adidas, Allbirds, and Veja.

The biggest issues are:

Weak product presentation

Lack of real e‑commerce UX patterns

Low visual hierarchy

Missing trust‑building elements

Inconsistent naming and product taxonomy

No brand story or differentiation

These are all fixable — and improving them will dramatically increase the professionalism of the case study.
